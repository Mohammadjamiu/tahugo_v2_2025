Guest Post & Sponsored Post Opportunities

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Tooabstractive.com! We welcome collaboration with companies and fellow bloggers. Below, you'll find our guidelines and policies for guest posts and sponsored posts.

Guest Posts:

Guest posts are an excellent opportunity for bloggers to share their expertise and connect with our audience. Here's what you need to know:

  1. Content Relevance: We focus on providing diverse content related to engineering, technology, writing, productivity, and financial topics. Please ensure your guest post aligns with these subjects to provide value to our readers.
  2. Topics: Please email us with 2-3 suggested topics that are relevant to our readership. Craft your guest post with a purposeful and engaging approach, keeping it authentic and tailored to our audience's interests.
  3. Links and Bio: Include up to two links to your website within the guest post, along with an author's bio. We encourage you to link to relevant articles on tooabstractive.com as well.

Sponsored Posts:

Sponsored posts offer companies and services the opportunity to gain exposure to our engaged audience. Here are the details:

  1. Customized Packages: We offer tailored sponsored post packages that include maximum exposure on tooabstractive.com. Contact us for further information and pricing details.
  2. SEO Optimization: Our sponsored posts are optimized for search engines to increase visibility and reach, helping your content rank higher in relevant searches.

Criteria for Posting:

To ensure the quality and relevance of content on tooabstractive.com, we have the following criteria:

  1. Originality: All content must be original and exclusive to tooabstractive.com. We do not accept plagiarized or previously published content.
  2. Content Quality: Maintain high-quality standards by running your post through grammar and readability tools like Grammarly and Hemingway. Craft clear, concise, and engaging content.
  3. Length: Aim for a minimum of 1,000 words, with no maximum limit. Longer articles of around 1,500-2,000 words tend to perform well.
  4. Formatting and Photos: Format your post with proper headings and include relevant links. Please provide copyright-free images or your own related photos.
  5. Promotion: We encourage guest post participants to promote their articles on social media platforms and link to their prior contributions on tooabstractive.com.

We appreciate your dedication to providing valuable insights to our readers. For submission or further inquiries, please contact us at connect@tooabstractive.com.

We look forward to considering your contribution and fostering a vibrant community of knowledge-sharing on tooabstractive.com!
